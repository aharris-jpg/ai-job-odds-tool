Methodology

This tool combines a national occupation-level AI exposure baseline with state labor-market data to estimate how implied odds may shift locally.

  • National implied odds are based on each job’s underlying AI exposure score and converted into an estimated probability scale.
  • State-adjusted implied odds start with the national baseline and then apply a local modifier using state wage, projected growth, annual openings, and occupational concentration.
  • Higher projected growth and stronger annual openings tend to reduce implied replacement pressure, while higher wages and stronger concentration can modestly increase it.
  • State rankings are editorial estimates built from these combined signals, not official government AI replacement forecasts.
